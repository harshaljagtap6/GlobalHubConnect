Frequently Asked Questions

Our housing assistance program provides guidance on finding suitable accommodation within your budget. We offer resources for searching rental listings, negotiating with landlords, and understanding lease agreements. Our social integration programs include a variety of activities and events designed to help you connect with other students and engage with the local community. From language exchange meetups to cultural excursions and volunteer opportunities, we offer a range of activities to suit diverse interests.
